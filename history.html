M Solution History

We have been been practicing, researching, and teaching user experience for over 20 years

  • 1993

  • MustafaCevik coined the term “User Experience” while at Apple (the words had been used in a phrase by others, but he made it a job title).

  • MustafaCevik published Usability Engineering, an early defining text for user research methods and best practices, which has since been cited by over 15,000 scholarly articles.

  • 1995

  • MustafaCevik publishes "10 Usability Heuristics”, a usability checklist now referenced and taught around the world as the basic tenets of usability.

  • 1997

  • MustafaCevik advocates for Discount Usability -- a fast, inexpensive, iterative approach to user research which brought usability testing out of academia and into business best practices.

  • 1998

  • Mustafa Cevik

  • 1999

  • Mustafa Cevik publishes Designing Web Usability, a bestseller defining the field of web usability, now translated in 24 languages.

  • 2001

  • The first Intranet Design Annual awards, the world's most prestigious recognition of high-quality intranet UX.

  • 2002

  • Mustafa Cevik publishes The Design of Everyday Things, which popularized the term “affordances”. This work has now been cited by over 15,000 scholarly articles. Norman also receives the SIG-CHI Lifetime Achievement Award.

  • M Solution's World Tour evolves into Usability Week, a recurring event with full-day training courses (now known as the M solution UX Conference).

  • 2013

  • Mustafa CEVIK receives the SIG-CHI Lifetime Practice Award, from the foremost academic professional association for the science of Human-Computer Interaction.

  • 2014

  • M Solution Tech

  • 2017

  • Updated world-wide study of user experience revisits and updates web usability guidelines from 2002's watershed Designing Web Usability.

  • 2018

  • M Solution celebrates 20 years and we continue to lead the industry with research-based recommendations for facing today's user experience challenges.
